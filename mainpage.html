Student Regulation : Chapter 3

Students Rights and Obligation

STUDENTS RIGHTS

1.Participants in academic and non - academic activities condcuted both inside and outside of school, including online and onsite formats

2.Receive religious education in accordance with Christian values

3.Utilize all school facilities appropriately, according to their intended functions and regulations

4.Receive equal treatment from the school principal, teachers, and staff

5.Access guidance and counseling services from counselors or teachers based on needs and procedures

6.Receive health service

7.Respond to and defend against any allegations made toward them.

8.Develop themselves by participating in Co-Curricular activities(CCA)provided by the school

9.Take remedial or makeup assesments as approved by subject teachers based on needs and guidelines.

10.Receive feedback or results an assigments or assessments within two weeks at the latest

STUDENTS OBLIGATION

1.Understand and uphold the school's core values:Love, Peace, Patience, Kindness, Goodness, Faithfullness, Gentleness, and Self Control

2.Comply with all school rules, both written and verbal.

3.Show respect to all members of the school community through polite behaviour and speech.

4.Maintain the good reputation of the school both on and off campus, including on social media.

5.Be present at school on time during in person learning.

6.Participate in all classes and school programs

7.Inform and request permission hwhen absent:five consecutive unexcused absences are considered a withdrawal

8.Ask for clarification or updates on lessons or school activities that were missed or not understood.

9.Come prepared with books and learning tools as required

10.Actively participate in school events and activities.
